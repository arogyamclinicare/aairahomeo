## üìê Technical Specifications

When using Google Stitch, specify:
- **Aspect Ratio**: 
  - Hero images: 4:3 or 16:10 (landscape)
  - Icons: 1:1 (square)
  - Backgrounds: 16:9 (wide)
- **Style**: Modern medical illustration, photorealistic, or minimalist flat design
- **Color Palette**: Emerald green (#10b981), Teal (#14b8a6), Soft cyan, White
- **Resolution**: High resolution (at least 1920x1080 for hero, 512x512 for icons)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background (for icons), JPG for photos

---

## üí° Pro Tips for Google Stitch

1. **Be Specific**: Include details about style (flat design, illustration, photo-realistic)
2. **Color Keywords**: Always mention "emerald green", "teal", "natural healing colors"
3. **Mood**: Add words like "warm", "professional", "approachable", "trustworthy"
4. **Medical Context**: Mention "homeopathic", "natural medicine", "holistic healing"
5. **Quality**: Request "high resolution", "8k", "professional quality"
6. **Background**: Specify "white background", "transparent", or "subtle gradient"
